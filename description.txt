Reverie is a mod that aims to add as many layers as an onion to Terraria's sandbox formula. By introducing a variety of subworlds, Unique and interactive NPCs, missions and mini-boss hunts, Reverie adds "Looter Shooter" elements into Terraria's formula.


Composer(s): Signiaa (wymsical)
Lead Artist: ElectroManiac
Contributing Members: HugeKraken
